Zhongan Online Property Insurance Co., Ltd. was jointly established by Ant Financial, Ping An of China, and Tencent in 2013.

众安在线财产保险股份有限公司由蚂蚁金服、中国平安和腾讯于2013年联合发起设立。

It focuses on reshaping the insurance value chain with new technologies, centering around four major ecosystems: health, digital life, consumer finance, and automobiles. It aims to serve the new generation with technology, providing personalized, customized, and intelligent new insurance.

专注于应用新技术重塑保险价值链，围绕健康、数字生活、消费金融、汽车四大生态，以科技服务新生代，为其提供个性化、定制化、智能化的新保险。

I interned in the Experience Design Team at Zhongan Data Science Application Center, and during this process, the more refined design thinking and systematic report summaries have greatly enlightened me.

我在众安数据科学应用中心的体验设计团队实习，在这个过程中，更加精细的设计思考和系统的报告总结让我受教匪浅。
