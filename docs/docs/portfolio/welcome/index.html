SEPHORA is a French cosmetics brand founded in 1969. The 2020 Global Top 500 Most Valuable Brands list was released, with Sephora ranking 170th.

During the past half year at Sephora, I worked as a UED intern in Digital Vnture Department.In there,our team feels like a warm family, creating beauty power and helping customers explore their different facets of beauty.

在丝芙兰的近半年的日子里，我在DV部门里担任一名UED实习生。在这里，我们的队伍像是一个温暖的大家庭，一起在创造美力和帮助顾客发现自己多面的美的路上砥砺前行。

Our work is divided into two parts: APP/MNP design and Future Store digital media design, and I have been involved in both，Including user research, UX and UI design.

我们的工作分为APP和MNP的设计以及未来店的数字媒体设计，而这两者我也均有参与。包括用户研究、交互设计和界面设计。
